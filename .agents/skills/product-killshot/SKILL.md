# Product killshot

Understand the product and target user, identify the most relevant competitor with current evidence, compare the core user journey and differentiation, then prioritize opportunities by user value, defensibility, effort, and evidence strength. Distinguish sourced facts from inference.

Read [references/full-guide.md](references/full-guide.md) when producing the full report or applying its scoring and output template.
